
# Teos Bankchain Mobile — SDG & Impact Mapping

This document maps Teos Bankchain Mobile modules to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), showing how the platform enables secure, inclusive, and regulator‑aligned financial infrastructure.

---

## SDG Summary Table

| SDG   | Focus                                | Module Alignment                                      | Primary Impact                          |
|-------|--------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| SDG 1 | No Poverty                           | Subsidy routing, benefit verification                 | Financial access for underserved groups  |
| SDG 8 | Decent Work & Economic Growth        | Payroll rails, SME onboarding, mobile wallets         | Economic inclusion and growth            |
| SDG 9 | Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure| Blockchain rails, mobile banking, crypto gateways     | Resilient financial infrastructure       |
| SDG 10| Reduced Inequalities                 | Inclusive KYC, remote onboarding, multilingual UX     | Equity in access and participation       |
| SDG 11| Sustainable Cities & Communities     | Smart‑city payments, emergency disbursement flows     | Urban financial resilience               |
| SDG 16| Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions | Audit trails, AML/CFT, regulator reporting            | Trust, transparency, legal compliance    |
| SDG 17| Partnerships for the Goals           | Bank/central bank integrations, global standards      | Scalable multi‑stakeholder collaboration |

---

## SDG 1 — No Poverty
- **Subsidy routing:** Verified eligibility flows for government and NGO disbursements.  
- **Benefit verification:** Blockchain‑based proof of income, identity, and need.  
- **Mobile access:** Offline‑capable wallets for rural and underserved populations.

---

## SDG 8 — Decent Work & Economic Growth
- **Payroll rails:** Transparent, programmable salary flows for public and private sectors.  
- **SME onboarding:** Fast KYC and mobile‑first banking for small businesses.  
- **Wallet infrastructure:** Secure, inclusive mobile wallets with biometric access.

---

## SDG 9 — Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure
- **Blockchain rails:** ISO 20022‑aligned crypto‑fiat connectors for banks and central banks.  
- **Mobile banking:** Secure onboarding, encrypted transactions, and biometric flows.  
- **Gateway modules:** ETH, Pi, BTC adapters with toggled compliance enforcement.

---

## SDG 10 — Reduced Inequalities
- **Inclusive KYC:** Low‑friction onboarding for unbanked and underbanked users.  
- **Remote access:** Mobile‑first flows with offline sync and low‑bandwidth support.  
- **Multilingual UX:** Arabic, English, and local language support for accessibility.

---

## SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities & Communities
- **Smart‑city payments:** Parking, transit, and utility flows linked to civic wallets.  
- **Emergency disbursement:** Rapid aid routing during crises or disasters.  
- **Urban resilience:** Blockchain‑based coordination for municipal finance.

---

## SDG 16 — Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions
- **Audit trails:** Immutable logs for every transaction, role, and consent.  
- **AML/CFT enforcement:** Real‑time screening, risk scoring, and sanctions compliance.  
- **Regulator reporting:** Automated exports for CBE, FATF, ECB, OFAC, SAMA, FCA.

---

## SDG 17 — Partnerships for the Goals
- **Bank integrations:** API connectors for commercial banks, central banks, and fintechs.  
- **Global standards:** Alignment with IMF, BIS, UNODC, and ISO frameworks.  
- **Multi‑stakeholder scale:** Modular deployment across jurisdictions and institutions.

---

## Measurement & Audit
- **Impact KPIs:** Financial inclusion rates, transaction volume, compliance flags, audit findings.  
- **Data quality:** Timeliness, accuracy, and completeness checks with automated alerts.  
- **Independent review:** Exportable reports and verifier roles for regulators and partners.
