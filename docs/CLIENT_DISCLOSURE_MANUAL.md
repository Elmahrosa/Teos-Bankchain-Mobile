### 2. Risks & Limitations
- **Market Risk:** Crypto values may fluctuate; TEOS does not guarantee asset stability.  
- **Compliance Risk:** Transactions subject to KYC/AML checks; flagged activity may be reported.  
- **Operational Risk:** Service availability may be impacted during protocol upgrades.  
- **Jurisdictional Limits:** Services restricted to approved regions under regulator agreements.  

---

### 3. Client Obligations
- Complete KYC verification before accessing services.  
- Agree to AML monitoring and potential SAR reporting.  
- Maintain secure access credentials (MFA required).  
- Accept treasury contribution fees (default 0.25%).  

---

### 4. Transparency & Reporting
- All transactions logged in immutable audit trails.  
- Clients may request transaction history exports (CSV/JSON).  
- Regulator reporting may include anonymized client data when required.  
- Treasury contributions and staking rewards disclosed quarterly.  

---

### 5. Governance & Voting (Institutional Clients)
- Institutional clients may mint NFT governance tokens.  
- Voting rights proportional to staked governance tokens.  
- Governance decisions may impact fees, compliance modules, or protocol upgrades.  
- Results published transparently via `/governance/results`.
