## [1.0.0] — 2025‑11‑30
### Added
- Initial release of **TEOS BankChain Mobile**.  
- FastAPI backend with modular routers and OpenAPI docs.  
- Expo/React Native mobile client with bank‑grade authentication.  
- CI/CD pipelines (`ci.yml`, `verify.yml`, `codeql.yml`).  
- Compliance templates (KYC/AML, regulator reporting, audit trails).  
- NDA and proprietary license enforcement.  

### Changed
- README.md unified with Documentation & Resources, Quickstart, and NDA.  
- CONTRIBUTING.md and CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md added for partner governance.  
- SECURITY.md finalized with reporting and enforcement protocols.  

### Fixed
- Broken links in documentation.  
- Duplicate license sections removed.  
- Compliance bullet formatting corrected.
